## Design Context

### Users
General users doing quick, casual file shares. No tech knowledge required.

### Brand Personality
Monolithic industrial - solid, weighty, precise. Emotions: "secure", "fast", "ease of use". Three-word personality: secure-fast-effortless.

### Aesthetic Direction
Minimal sleek with shades of grey. Light mode. Concrete-like neutrals. No color, just tone. Typography: distinctive display font paired with refined body.

### Design Principles
1. Shades of grey only - no accent colors
2. Snappy, immediate interactions - no perceived delay
3. Everything feels intentional and engineered
4. Generous spacing, tight hierarchy
5. Trust through simplicity and precision
